About Me

My name is Youssef Elkawas, and I am a high school student. I don't want to repeat myself; I don't want to just learn programming. I want to become a real builder who can create useful products and solve meaningful problems. I really want to add value that represents me and my skills. For me, it's not just about code or a career; it is about the meaning of it and the added value that can make a difference.

My background

I completed Harvard's CS50x, where I learned computer science fundamentals such as C, Python, SQL, Flask, and problem-solving. After that, I decided to focus on web development and started my journey toward full-stack engineering.

My Philosophy

I believe that building is the best way to learn, and words are nothing without a real trial. Therefore, you see most people say, 'I can swim' by just touching the water or seeing it in pictures. So, for every new concept I study, I try to apply it in a project. My goal is to focus on real understanding, consistent practice, and long-term improvement.

My Vision

My long-term vision is to grow from developer to builder to founder by building real systems and SaaS products, and eventually creating my own technology company. This will not happen by snapping my fingers; it is a long journey that will require continuous learning, building, and sharing. I am committed to this path and excited about the future possibilities.
