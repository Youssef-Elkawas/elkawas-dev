Post 1: My First Blog Post

After I complete the CS50x course, I didn't know what I should do next or what the next step was. I saw many courses, and everyone says, 'You will have a big loss if you miss this course.' I got distracted, but I grabbed a pen and paper and wrote down exactly what I want to do and where exactly I want to go. I wrote it in simple language. I found from CS50x that I am interested in the Web Development field. So, this is what I want to do, and I found that I want to create websites that solve real problems by making them from A to Z. So, this is who I want to be: a Full-Stack Developer. But also, the road isn't completely clear to me. By using AI, I drew a simple roadmap containing simple steps because I realized that I can't draw it clearly or predict it; I will know the path when I actually move along it.
