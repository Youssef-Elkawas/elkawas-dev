Post 1: My First Blog Post

After completing the CS50x course, I didn't know what to do next or what the next step was. I saw many courses, and everyone said, 'You will have a big loss if you miss this course.' I got distracted, but I grabbed a pen and paper and wrote down exactly what I wanted to do and where exactly I wanted to go. I wrote it in simple language. I found through CS50x that I am interested in web development. So, I decided that I want to create websites that solve real problems by building them from A to Z. This is who I want to be: a Full-Stack Developer. Since the road wasn't completely clear to me, I used AI to draw a simple roadmap with clear steps. I realized that I couldn't predict the whole path; I will know it as I move along. The first step was HTML, next is CSS...
